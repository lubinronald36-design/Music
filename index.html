A beautiful girl is like sunrise—
soft, warm, and full of promise.

Her smile is a quiet magic
that turns ordinary moments into gold.

Her presence colors the world
like sunlight spilling over morning hills.

There is grace in her steps,
poetry in her silence,
and whole galaxies in her eyes.

She is beauty that does not demand attention—
it simply exists.

And the world is softer
just because she is in it.
